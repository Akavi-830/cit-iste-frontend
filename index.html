About CIT 9.0

What is CIT?

Cognitive Intelligence Test is a multi-round competition designed to assess analytical, logical, and decision-making abilities.

Competition Structure

The contest includes a preliminary aptitude round followed by a problem-based final pitching round.

Why Participate?

CIT helps you develop structured thinking, teamwork, and real-world problem-solving skills.
